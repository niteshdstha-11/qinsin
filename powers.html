Powers and Abilities

Immense Cursed Energy

Sukuna possesses one of the largest cursed energy reserves in the series. His raw energy output surpasses most special-grade sorcerers and cursed spirits, allowing him to dominate battles effortlessly.

Domain Expansion – Malevolent Shrine

Sukuna's Domain Expansion, Malevolent Shrine, is unique because it does not require a closed barrier. Within its effective range, slashing attacks automatically strike all targets. This demonstrates Sukuna’s superior control and mastery over domain techniques.

Cleave and Dismantle

These are Sukuna’s primary offensive techniques. Dismantle delivers powerful slashes, while Cleave adjusts its strength according to the opponent’s durability, ensuring maximum damage.

Reverse Cursed Technique

Sukuna can heal himself using reverse cursed energy. He has demonstrated the ability to regenerate limbs and recover from severe injuries, making him extremely difficult to defeat.

Strategic Intelligence

Beyond physical power, Sukuna is a highly intelligent and analytical fighter. He carefully studies his opponents, understands their techniques quickly, and exploits weaknesses with precision.
