Personality

Ryomen Sukuna is arrogant, sadistic, and highly confident in his abilities. He views most humans and sorcerers as insignificant and enjoys demonstrating his superiority. Despite his cruelty, he possesses a sharp intellect and deep understanding of jujutsu principles.

Unlike mindless curses, Sukuna acts with purpose and strategy. He rarely wastes energy and chooses his actions carefully, making him one of the most dangerous antagonists in the series.
