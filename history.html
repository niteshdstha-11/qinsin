History and Legacy


Ryomen Sukuna's history is shrouded in mystery and legend. During the Heian Era, Sukuna was considered an unstoppable force. Multiple powerful sorcerers united in an attempt to defeat him, yet his strength proved overwhelming. After his eventual downfall, his cursed energy remained so potent that his body could not be destroyed.



As a result, his remains were preserved as twenty cursed fingers, each containing a fragment of his immense power.
